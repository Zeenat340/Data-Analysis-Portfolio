# Power BI Projects

## Merchandise Sales Analysis — Onyx Data Community Challenge
- **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI
- **Skills demonstrated:** Data modelling, DAX measures, interactive 
  dashboards, slicers, custom visuals

### Key Insights
- Business generates strong revenue from international customers 
  who represent the majority of sales
- Ratings are consistent across all product categories 
  averaging between 3.4 and 3.6
- No repeat purchases recorded suggesting customer retention 
  is a key area for improvement
- Monthly revenue trend shows consistent performance across 2023 and 2024

## Farmers Financial Analysis — Independent Project
- **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI
- **Dataset:** Agricultural farmers dataset
- **Skills demonstrated:** Data modelling, DAX measures, KPI cards,
  scatter plots, treemaps, slicers, bar charts

### Key Insights
- Total farmer income across all states is $11bn
- Benue and Kano are the highest income generating states
- Crop farming dominates with 4.83K acres compared to 
  Livestock at 2.69K and Mixed at 1.81K

## Forggith Drug Sales Dashboard — Guided Project
- **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI
- **Skills demonstrated:** Multi-page report design, DAX measures,
  YTD, PYTD, SPLY calculations, page navigation, slicers,
  treemaps, area charts, KPI cards with percentage change

### Key Insights
- Total revenue of €11.12bn significantly exceeds total target of €8bn
- YTD revenue of €2.7bn reflects 31.4% growth from last year
- Pharmacy channel generates slightly more revenue at €5.88bn 
  compared to Hospital at €5.24bn
